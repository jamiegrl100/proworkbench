# Code of Conduct

This project uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or insults
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking corrective action.
